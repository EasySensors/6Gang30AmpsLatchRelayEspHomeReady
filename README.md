# About

6 Gang 30 Amps Latch Relays in DIN Rail enclosure. 6 Optron isolated inputs, 2 inputs for DS18b20 Dallas 1-Wire temperature sensors.  EspHomeReady.


ESP32-S2 Module pinout

Latch Relays ESP32-S2 pins:
Latch Relay 1 ON - 10
Latch Relay 1 OFF - 9
Latch Relay 2 ON - 20
Latch Relay 2 OFF - 3
Latch Relay 3 ON - 19
Latch Relay 3 OFF - 8
Latch Relay 4 ON - 15
Latch Relay 4 OFF - 16
Latch Relay 5 ON - 7
Latch Relay 5 OFF - 6
Latch Relay 6 ON - 5
Latch Relay 6 OFF - 4

Tact button ESP32-S2 pins:
Button 1 - 11
Button 2 - 12
Button 3 - 13
Button 4 - 14
Button 5 - 21
Button 6 - 33

Optocoupler ESP32-S2 switches:
Switch 1 - 34
Switch 2 - 35
Switch 3 - 36
Switch 4 - 37
Switch 5 - 38
Switch 6 - 39

Dallas sensor ESP32-S2 pins:
Temp 1 - 40
Temp 2 - 41

i2c expander ESP32-S2 (LED's pins):
SDA - 17
SCL - 18
